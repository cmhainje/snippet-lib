connor's snippet library

over the years, I've found that often I end up needing to solve a problem that I know I've solved before. sometimes I re-invent the same old solution; sometimes I can't quite figure it out on the second go. sometimes I can find the old code; sometimes I can't. no matter what, I'm wasting time trying to do something I know I've done before.

this snippet library is my attempt to solve this issue. whenever I find myself looking up how to do something for the third or fourth time, I'm just going to throw the solution up here. I'll try to make these snippets as short and dependency-free as possible, so they can be dropped into new projects without worry. maybe they will prove useful to you, too.

start by looking through the recents and tags, or go somewhere ! or if you're looking for something specific, try the search.
